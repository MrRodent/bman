How To Play


Objective

  • Strategically place bombs to defeat your opponents and
    obstacles while staying alive.

Controls

  • W: Move Up
  • A: Move Left
  • S: Move Down
  • D: Move Right
  • Space: Place Bomb

Bombs

  • Each bomb will explode after a short delay
  • The explosion can destroy some obstacles
  • If it touches an opponent, it will eliminate them
  • Be careful to not get caught in the explosion from your own bomb

Power-Ups

  • You may find power-ups throughout the level
  • These can include:
    • Increased bomb range
    • Additional bombs
    • Faster movement speed
  • Walk over the power-up to collect it

Enemies

  • Be cautious of your opponents movements
  • Each opponent moves differently

Level Completion

  • Eliminate all opponents, find the door and go through it.
